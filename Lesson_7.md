* Try and error -- guess 
* An intelligent guess -- Sinusoidal curve 
    * $\sin'(x) = \cos(x)$
    * $\cos'(x) = -\sin(x)$
    * $\sin''(x) = -\sin(x)$
* more
    * $\sin'(\omega x) = \omega \cos(\omega x)$
    * $\sin''(\omega x) = -\omega^2 \sin(\omega x)$
