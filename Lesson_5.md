## Lesson 5 - Work and Energy - 7/17/2022

* $F = m a$ 
* Vector and scalar
    * $\vec{F} = m \vec{a}$
    * $F_x = m a_x$, $F_y = m a_y$, $F_z = m a_y$
* Infinitesimal -- dx, dt 
    * $\vec{a} = d\vec{v}/dt$  - $\vec{a} = \Delta \vec{v}/ \Delta t$
    * $\vec{F} = m d\vec{v}/dt = d(m\vec{v})/dt = d\vec{p}/dt$
* Momentum
    *\vec{p} = m \vec{v}$
